Evolution of Thought

From strict mechanics to human relations.

Era 1

Scientific Management

Focus on laws, production, and physical efficiency. Workers as parts of a machine.

1924

Hawthorne Studies

The shift to the Social Aspect. Proved that attention to workers and social factors increases productivity more than physical conditions.

Era 2

Chester Barnard

Zone of Indifference: Workers comply if orders don't conflict with personal interests.

Era 3

Mary Parker Follett

Conflict Resolution & Integration. Both parties find a mutually satisfying solution rather than compromise.

The "It Depends" Principle

The Contingency School teaches us that there is no single "best way" to manage.

Management Response
